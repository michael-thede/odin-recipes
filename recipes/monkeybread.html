Monkey Bread

Return to Home

Description

This dessert consists of many bits of premade biscuit that are covered in a mixture of brown sugar, butter, and cinnamon. You eat it by pulling off chunks from the main body, making it a fun experience and a delicious treat!

Ingredients

  • 4 cans of 10 biscuits each
  • 1 tablespoon of cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 cups of brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 sticks of butter
  • Optional: 1/2 cup chopped pecans or raisins

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F.
  2. Cut biscuits into fourths and coat with sugat and cinnamon mixture.
  3. In saucepan, melt 1 1/2 sticks butter, 1 1/2 cups brown sugar, and 1 tablespoon cinnamon.
  4. Place 1/2 biscuits in greased Bundt pan.
  5. Add 1/2 cup chopped pecans or raisins.
  6. Pour half of the melted liquid over this.
  7. Repeat with remaining ingredients and bake for 35-40 minutes at 350 F.
